About Us

Valent Projects is a specialist strategic communications consultancy. We help our clients reach, understand and move their audiences.

The world around us is changing rapidly. At the heart of this change, is the way people learn about the world, express themselves and engage with each other. Our mission is to understand this disruption and to develop new strategies and tactics to navigate the fast evolving landscape.

Our Work

In an increasingly interconnected world, we believe there is no substitute for engaging people directly. The days when any one broadcaster or publication could reach a huge mass of people and inform their opinions are long gone. Our world is much more democratic. However, even though anyone with an internet connection can become a broadcaster, to be heard above the clamour requires an understanding of the audience, the context and the media landscape.

Whoever we are working with, we work to create valence — finding that common ground over which people can combine their efforts and together build something greater than the sum of its parts.

  • Research

    We conduct research to understand where audiences are getting their information and what they are talking about. Key to our approach is to understand people’s narratives so clients can get insight into perhaps the most important question; why?

  • Strategy

    We work with individuals and organisations to help them clarify what they want to achieve and – critically – how.

  • Engagement

    Whether running media offices, campaigns, developing media content or advising leaders, we run projects in a way that combines cutting edge techniques with local knowledge and practical skill.
